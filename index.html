Chapter 1: The Journey Begins

It all started when my darling three year old asked about the beach as we watched videos on YouTube. She inquired about the sun, water, and the animals beneath the water. Oh but she wasn't ready for such a long long drive!

Chapter 2: Sand and Sunshine

We arrived late but that didn't stop us from visiting the beach. We walked along the water, collected a few seashells, and burried Daddy in the sand.

Chapter 3

We got up with the sun and hit the beach to find sea shells that might have washed up overnight (gotta get first pick!)

Chapter 4

On day three, we explored all the hotel had to offer. We started with breakfast at the restaurant downstairs. next, we headed to the pool and splash park area. we met some friends whom we invited for our birthday festivities the next day. in the evening they had a campfire where we enjoyed s'mores with new friends!

Chapter 5

It's Birthday Day! We got ourselves together and headed to the store for cake and ice cream to share with our friends.

Chapter 6

If homesick was a person it would be the little lady! But Gulf World awaited us that day, and she soon forgot about missing home.
